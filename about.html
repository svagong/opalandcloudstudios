About me

Who am I?

A Bay Area native, living in San Jose. I currently work as a Lab Manager at a biotech start-up in Menlo Park, California (the heart of west-coast biotech.) I studied as a researcher in San Diego, and came back to the Bay after receiving my masters in Environmental Science. I love experiencing the world through the eyes of a scientist: conducting field research, teaching community science, and running experiments, but in the process found huge gaps in communication between researchers and the public. Science can be intimidating, and a huge chunk of my time as a researcher was spent simplifying information into digestible and appealing bites to be communicated to a wide audience. These were also my favorite parts, and coupled with my long-standing hobbies of graphic design and photography, drew me towards UI design.

I like to say I specialize in information: making it, distilling it, and communicating it. Even now in my role as a manager, I spend most of my time creating and managing Notion and Excel templates for displaying and tracking crucial project and operations data. I minor in problem solving, having participated as a founding member in two tiny and ambitious start-ups for the past six years.
