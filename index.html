About me

A Bay Area native, living in San Jose. I currently freelance as a UI designer and photographer, and work as a Lab Manager at a biotech start-up in the Bay Area, California. I studied as a researcher in San Diego, and came back to the Bay after receiving my masters in Environmental Science. I love experiencing the world through the eyes of a scientist: conducting field research, teaching community science, and running experiments, but found huge gaps in communication between researchers and the public. Science can be intimidating, and a huge chunk of my time as a researcher was spent simplifying information into digestible and appealing bites to be communicated to a wide audience. These were also my favorite parts, and coupled with my long-standing hobbies of graphic design and photography, drew me towards UI design. I love helping people communicate their ideas, via website, graphics, or photography, and would love to chat about your next big project!
